About

Welcome to Probability Turtle.

This site was simply built with nostalgia in mind. The goal was to build a functional website imitating the browser, time-waster type games of the 2000's.

I took specific inspiration from a tik-tok auto which posed the question:

"If you had the opportunity to press a button for $25,000, but there was a 1% chance you'd turn into a turtle, how many times would you press it?"
